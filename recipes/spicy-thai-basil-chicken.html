Spicy Thai Basil Chicken

Description

This recipe was created by John Mitzewich. If you don't have Thai or holy basil, it can still be made with regular basil. The sauce acts like a glaze as the chicken mixture cooks over high heat. The recipe works best if you chop or grind your own chicken and have all ingredients prepped before you start cooking.

Give yourself 15 mins to prep and 10 mins to cook, for a total of 25 mins. The dish serves 2 and contains 715 calories, 30g of fat, 59g of carbs, and 50g of protein per serving.
